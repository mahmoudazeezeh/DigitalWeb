Digital Logic design

Digital logic design is the foundation of modern electronic systems, using logic gates (AND, OR, NOT) to convert inputs into logical outputs. This includes building combinational and sequential circuits, contributing to the development of smart devices and central processing units, and relies on Boolean algebra.
